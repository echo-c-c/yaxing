Lushan Yaxing Technology Development Co., Ltd.

Lushan Yaxing Technology Development Co., Ltd. is a specialized company engaged in the research, design, production, sales, and engineering services of carbon materials for blast furnaces and submerged arc furnaces. The company is ISO 9001 International Quality Management System certified.

Adhering to the philosophy of "high-quality products and excellent services" based on customer needs, Lushan Yaxing collaborates with clients with integrity, striving to provide higher-quality carbon bricks and supporting refractory materials for the efficient and long-lasting operation of blast furnaces and submerged arc furnaces worldwide. Additionally, we continuously develop new long-life furnace lining designs and innovative carbon brick products to meet customers' practical requirements. By implementing strict and refined quality control at every stage of production and leveraging innovative process designs and modern production equipment, our products have been successfully applied in over 1,200 blast furnaces ranging from 450m³ to 5,800m³ both domestically and internationally.

Our specialized carbon bricks for submerged arc furnaces have been used in over 1,000 furnaces with a capacity exceeding 10,000 KVA, covering industries such as industrial silicon, ferronickel, ferrosilicon, ferrochrome, high-carbon ferrochrome, high-carbon ferromanganese, calcium carbide, and yellow phosphorus production. Our blast furnace and submerged arc furnace products are used by customers across the globe, making us one of China's leading manufacturers of high-quality carbon bricks for blast furnaces and submerged arc furnaces.

Main Carbon Products

  • Semi-graphitic carbon bricks
  • Graphite bricks
  • High thermal conductivity carbon bricks
  • Microporous carbon bricks
  • Ultra-microporous carbon bricks
  • High thermal conductivity & corrosion-resistant carbon bricks
  • Specialized carbon bricks for various ferroalloy submerged arc furnaces
  • High thermal conductivity graphite-based and carbon-based ramming materials, castables, and carbon cement
